Tip: Hold down the Option key to preview changes
  • Enter regular expressions to match URLs for which Safari Reader will be automatically activated.
  • Place each expression on its own line.
  • It's not necessary to escape literal slashes. Literal dots should be escaped.
Examples:
//www\.nytimes\.com/\d{4}/\d{2}/\d{2}(/.*)+/.+\.html
//www\.macworld\.com/.+/\d+/.+
